# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Clients planning significant life events: couples planning weddings, families organizing cultural or religious celebrations, and corporate teams commissioning events. They come with high expectations, are making emotionally and financially meaningful decisions, and need to feel confident they're in expert hands from the first glance at the site.

## Product Purpose

Nakshatra Events is a full-service event planning company handling weddings, corporate events, cultural and religious celebrations, and social gatherings. The site exists to attract new clients, communicate premium quality and deep expertise, and drive inquiries. Success means a visitor arrives, feels the brand's caliber immediately, and reaches out.

## Brand Personality

Refined, commanding, celebratory. The brand carries the weight of Indian heritage with a contemporary luxury sensibility — not maximalist or flashy, but deeply confident. The aesthetic is rich without being gaudy, elegant without being cold.

## Anti-references

- Over-the-top bling: excessive gold gradients, glitter effects, sparkle animations — flashy is not refined
- Generic Western wedding sites: pale pastels, watercolor florals, no cultural identity
- Busy event portals: overwhelming listing grids, cluttered navigation, directory aesthetics

## Design Principles

1. **Earned luxury** — every visual choice signals quality through restraint and precision, not decoration for its own sake.
2. **Cultural confidence** — Indian heritage is the identity, not the accent. The visual language should feel rooted, not applied.
3. **Presence before function** — the first impression is emotional. The site should command attention before explaining anything.
4. **Every detail intentional** — an event planner's credibility is their eye for detail; the site must demonstrate that same precision.
5. **Show the work** — imagery, case studies, and specific event details earn trust more than claims about "world-class service."

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Rich color palettes must maintain 4.5:1 body text contrast. Motion effects must respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. The audience spans generations; type sizing should be generous.
